## 📝 NOTES & REFERENCES

### SEO Best Practices to Remember
- Keyword placement: Title (most important) → Meta description → H1 → Content
- Never keyword stuff - maintain natural, readable content
- Focus on user intent, not just keywords
- Create comprehensive content (2,000+ words for main topics)
- Update old content regularly to maintain freshness signal
- Internal linking helps distribute authority
- Backlinks from authoritative sites are gold
